Honest Answers

The questions every smart pool buyer asks before they apply for financing.

How much can I qualify for?

Lyon Financial offers loans up to $250,000 for qualified buyers. Your actual amount depends on credit profile, income, and existing debt. Pre-qualifying through Lyon's application takes about 5 minutes and shows you exactly what you'd be approved for — before you ever commit to a build.

Will applying hurt my credit score?

No. The pre-qualification is a soft credit pull, which has zero impact on your credit score. A hard pull only happens if you decide to move forward and formally accept the loan offer. You can see your rate, then walk away with no consequence.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

Lyon Financial works with a wide range of credit profiles. They don't publish a fixed minimum, because they look at the full picture — income, debt-to-income, employment, and credit history together. The fastest way to know is to spend 5 minutes pre-qualifying. It costs you nothing.

Is this a HELOC, or an unsecured loan?

Lyon Financial offers both options. Most of our clients choose an unsecured pool loan, which doesn't put your home up as collateral. If you have substantial equity and prefer a secured product, they can structure that too. The application will walk you through which fits your situation.

How fast is the approval?

Most applicants get a pre-qualification decision within minutes of submitting the online form. Final approval after you choose to move forward typically lands within a few business days, depending on documentation. You can be designing your pool the same week you apply.

Can I pay it off early without penalty?

Yes. Lyon Financial loans have no prepayment penalty. You can pay extra each month, make lump-sum payments, or pay the whole thing off the day you close on a refinance. The 30-year term is a ceiling, not a commitment.

What if my pool ends up costing less than the loan amount?

You only borrow what you need. The loan funds disburse to the build in stages as work is completed. If your final invoice is lower than your approved amount, you simply borrow less. You don't pay interest on money you didn't use.

Does Florida Coastal Contractors see my credit or financial info?

No. The application goes directly to Lyon Financial. We don't see your credit score, income, or financial details. We're only notified that you're an approved buyer once you're cleared to fund. Your financial privacy stays between you and the lender.
